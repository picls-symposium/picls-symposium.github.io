PiCLS Symposium
23rd September 2015 at the West Park Conference Centre - Dundee

Welcome to the 6th Annual PiCLS Symposium.
The event is open to all staff and students and promises to be as exciting as previous years. We will have a wide range of renowned guest speakers covering a large spectrum of life sciences research.

Why should I Attend?

The PiCLS symposium is an outstanding occasion to expand your scientific perspectives and interests as well as an opportunity to meet top researchers.

Presented by Students

We are a group of PhD students from the University of Dundee (the committee 2015) and we have the pleasure to invite you to this year Symposium.

For Students

We highly encourage PhD students to participate in the Symposium by submitting their research. Prizes will be awarded to top posters and to the best student talk!

About

The College of Life Sciences’ PhD Association (PiCLS) within the University of Dundee was established in 2008 to allow networking between PhD students in different subject areas. In 2010 the PiCLS symposium was introduced and was such a great success that the event is now an annual symposium run entirely by students.
